A record here is public and cannot be recalled — that is the point, not a bug. Don't post anything private or anything you'd regret carving in stone. Your identity is a secret key on this device; back it up — anyone who has it can post as you, and losing it means losing the name. A record is durable, not eternal: it lives as long as one relay keeps a copy, and you can run that relay.
