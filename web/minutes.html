Clerk Minutes

Put your town on the record.

Sign your minutes, agenda, or legal notice. No vendor. No account. No recall.

0 characters

This signs your town's record with the Town Seal (a Nostr key) and broadcasts it to several independent relays. It is public and cannot be recalled: that is the point. Sign only text your body has already approved. The seal is a secret key. anyone who holds it can post as your town, so guard it and back it up. A record is durable, not eternal: it lives as long as one relay keeps a copy, and you can run that relay.
